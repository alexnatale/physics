Question 1 – Potential Energy of a Charge System

(a) Total potential energy U = k ∑i<j &frac{qiqj}{rij}:

  • r12 = 0.030 m, r13 = 0.040 m, r23 = 0.050 m
  • U = 8.99 × 109 [(5·−3)/(0.03) + (5·4)/(0.04) + (−3·4)/(0.05)] × 10−18 J

U ≈ −2.16 × 10−6 J

(b) Potential at (2 cm, 2 cm):

  • V = k (q1/r1 + q2/r2 + q3/r3) ≈ 1.65 × 103 V
  • Work bringing q4=+2.00 × 10−9C from ∞:
    W = q4V

W ≈ 3.31 × 10−6 J

Question 2 – Potential Difference in a Non‑Uniform Field

(a) ΔVAB = VB − VA = kQ(1/rB − 1/rA) ≈ −1.35 × 105 V

(b) Work done by the field: W = −qΔV

W ≈ 2.70 × 10−2 J

(c) Kinetic energy becomes ΔK = W.
Speed v = √(2ΔK/m) ≈ 1.0 × 103 m·s−1

Question 3 – Potential of a Charged Spherical Shell

(a) For r > R: V = kQ/r; for r < R: V = kQ/R (E = 0 inside).

(b) V(0.10 m) = 4.79 × 104 V; V(0.30 m) = 2.40 × 104 V.

(c) Inside the shell E = 0, so potential is spatially constant.

Question 4 – Potential on the Axis of a Charged Ring

(a) V(x) = \dfrac{kQ}{\sqrt{a^{2}+x^{2}}}

(b) V(0.10 m) ≈ 2.01 × 104 V;
V(0.50 m) ≈ 8.35 × 103 V.

(c) Setting V(x)=½V(0) gives x = √3 a ≈ 0.35 m.

Question 5 – Work & Energy in Electric Fields

(a) Electron: ΔK = eΔV = 5.00 keV (8.01 × 10−16 J);
v ≈ 4.19 × 107 m·s−1.

(b) Proton: same ΔK; v ≈ 9.79 × 105 m·s−1.

(c) Electron attains ≈40× greater speed owing to its ≈1836× smaller mass.

Question 6 – Electric Field from Potential

(a) V(x,y)=200x2y ⇒ Ex= −400xy,    Ey= −200x2.

(b) At (0.20 m,0.30 m): E = ⟨−24, −8⟩ V/m; |E| ≈ 25.3 V/m directed 18.4° below the −x axis.

(c) Field is conservative because it is derived from a scalar potential (curl E = 0).

Question 7 – Equipotential Surfaces

(a) Potential on x‑axis is zero where |x+0.15| = |x−0.15| ⇒ x = 0.

(b) At (0, 0.15 m) and (0, 0.30 m) the potentials cancel → V = 0. A positive test charge there experiences E toward +x and accelerates rightward.

(c) E‑field lines intersect equipotential surfaces at 90 ° because work along an equipotential is zero.

(a) Vsurface = kQ/R ≈ 3.60 × 104 V;
V(0.20 m) ≈ 1.80 × 104 V.

(b) If the sphere is hollow, external answers stay the same; the interior is field‑free and at Vsurface.

(c) Contact redistribution:
Q′big=2.67 × 10−7 C, Q′small=1.33 × 10−7 C;
Final potential ≈ 2.40 × 104 V for both spheres.
