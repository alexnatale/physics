R = R₀[1 + α(T - T₀)]
ρ = ρ₀[1 + α(T - T₀)]
Ohm's Law
V = IR
J = σE

DC Circuits
Kirchhoff's Rules
Junction rule: ∑Iin = ∑Iout
Loop rule: ∑V = 0
Resistors in Series

Resistors in Parallel

RC Circuits
Time Constant
τ = RC
Charging a Capacitor

Constants
